What is SmartScript.ai?

SmartScript.ai is an AI-powered research paper generator designed to help students and researchers quickly generate academic papers in formats such as IEEE, Springer, IJME, and more. Just provide your project summary and choose the publication type — the AI takes care of the rest.

Key Features

  • AI-based content generation tailored for academic writing
  • Supports multiple publication formats (IEEE, Springer, etc.)
  • Editable sections like Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, and Conclusion
  • Download the final paper as PDF or DOCX
  • Clean, responsive user interface

How to Use the App

Step 1: Open the SmartScript.ai homepage.
Step 2: Enter your short project summary or idea.
Step 3: Select the target journal or publication format (e.g., IEEE).
Step 4: Click on "Generate Paper" to let the AI create your formatted research paper.
Step 5: Review and edit the content if needed. Then export the document as PDF or DOCX.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vue.js or React)
  • Backend: Java with Spring Boot
  • AI Engine: GPT-4o
  • Exporting: PDFKit, Docx libraries

Future Enhancements

  • Citation support with tools like Zotero or Mendeley
  • Plagiarism detection integration
  • Collaborative writing and editing features
  • References auto-generation and bibliography formatting
